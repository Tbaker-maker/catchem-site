    Straight answers

    Questions we get asked a lot.

    Is my data really unlimited on free?
    Yes. Your collection, goal list, and market access are unlimited on the free plan — forever. Pro only caps intelligence tools (Grading ROI, full flip finder, unlimited multi-channel alerts). We're never capping your own data.
    Where does the market data come from?
    The official Pokémon TCG API for the full card catalog and live TCGplayer pricing — refreshed hourly. Sealed products come from our curated library of 56 items. Rotation status is verified directly from Pokémon.com's official rotation announcement.
    Does the card scanner actually work?
    The scanning UX is fully built — camera capture, breakdown screen, add-to-collection flow. Real card recognition is coming via the Pokémon TCG API. Today the demo mode picks from the live catalog so you can see the whole flow end-to-end.
    Can it watch Facebook Marketplace for me?
    Facebook doesn't allow third-party tools to monitor listings — and tools that tried got banned. What Catch'em does is generate the perfect pre-filtered FB search URL for each goal. You tap FB's native 🔔 bell icon once; FB handles the alerts. Same pattern for eBay (saved searches with email) and TCGplayer (per-product price alerts).
    Do I need to be in the US?
    No. Buying works worldwide. Selling via TCGplayer requires a US bank account & TIN — we flag that up front. eBay and Facebook selling works from anywhere with their native account requirements.
    How does rotation affect my collection?
    When a set rotates out of Standard (like April 2026 for G-mark), Pokémon typically winds down printing — which tightens supply over 12-24 months. Sets that weren't heavily reprinted (Evolving Skies, Fusion Strike) start climbing and reward patient collectors. Heavily-reprinted sets dip first before eventually recovering. Catch'em tracks this explicitly per set so you know what's worth adding to your long-term hold.
